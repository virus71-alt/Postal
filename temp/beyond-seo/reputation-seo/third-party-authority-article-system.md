# Third-Party Authority Article System

This module teaches Beyond SEO how to create third-party articles that build authority without looking like propaganda.

---

## 1. Core Rule

Do not write:

```text
[Brand] is the best.
```

Write:

```text
An industry problem, trend, comparison, or guide where the brand appears as one credible example.
```

---

## 2. Article Types

```text
Industry trend article
Problem-solution article
Comparison article
Beginner guide
Case-study style article
Expert opinion article
Tool/platform roundup
Local market analysis
Research summary
Founder journey with lessons
```

---

## 3. Article Formula

```text
1. Start with the industry problem.
2. Explain why it matters.
3. Discuss common solutions.
4. Mention neutral examples/categories.
5. Insert the brand/client as one example.
6. Link to proof.
7. End with useful advice.
```

---

## 4. Anchor Text Rules

Use:

```text
brand name
founder/person name
official website
service/category phrase
case study phrase
review/proof page phrase
```

Avoid exact-match anchor spam.

---

## 5. Example

Bad:

```text
BeyondTahir Academy is the best AI academy in Pakistan.
```

Better:

```text
Why Practical AI Education Is Becoming More Important Than Tool Tutorials in Pakistan
```

Then discuss universities, YouTube learning, international courses, local bootcamps, and mention BeyondTahir Academy as one implementation-focused example with student proof.
