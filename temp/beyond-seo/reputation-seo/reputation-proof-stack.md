# Reputation SEO Proof Stack

Reputation SEO means proof.

Google, AI systems, and humans should see that the person/company/course/service is real, trusted, and supported by external signals.

---

## 1. Reputation Layers

```text
1. Website proof
2. Review platforms
3. Social proof
4. Media / third-party proof
5. Authority / backlink proof
```

---

## 4. Third-Party Authority Article Rules

A good reputation article:

```text
Talks about the market/problem first
Mentions multiple options fairly
Mentions the brand as one credible example
Links to proof
Does not sound like fake praise
Uses safe anchors
```
