# Local Authority Links

This file defines safe local link-building for local SEO campaigns.

Local authority links often matter more than generic high-DA links for local service rankings.

---

## 1. Best Local Link Types

```text
Chamber of commerce
local business associations
industry associations
local sponsorships
community events
school/nonprofit sponsorship pages
local press stories
provider/manufacturer listings
local directories with real traffic
health/legal/professional profiles
partner/vendor pages
```

---

## 2. Medical/Dental Examples

```text
Chamber profile
Dental society profile
Healthgrades/Zocdoc/dental directories
implant manufacturer/provider listings
local community sponsorship
local press education story
clinic partner pages
```

---

## 3. Evaluation Checklist

```text
Is it locally relevant?
Is it niche relevant?
Is it indexed?
Is the page real and useful?
Does it have organic traffic or local trust?
Does it avoid spammy outbound links?
Can we use branded or natural anchors?
Is the price fair if paid?
```
