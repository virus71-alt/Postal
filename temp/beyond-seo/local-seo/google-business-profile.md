# Google Business Profile Optimization

This file defines Beyond SEO's Google Business Profile workflow for local SEO.

GBP can drive calls, directions, bookings, and local trust. For many local businesses, website SEO and GBP must work together.

---

## 1. When to Use

Use this file for:

```text
local businesses
clinics
dentists
doctors
law firms
gyms
restaurants
real estate offices
service-area businesses
agencies with local leads
```

---

## 2. GBP Audit Inputs

Ask for or inspect:

```text
Business name
GBP URL or screenshot
Primary category
Secondary categories
Services/products
Business description
Address/service area
Phone
Website link
Appointment link
Opening hours
Photos
Posts
Q&A
Reviews
GBP insights if available
UTM-tagged links
Competitor GBP profiles
```

If GBP access is unavailable, say:

```text
GBP performance is not verified. For local lead generation, this is a major missing data source.
```

---

## 3. Core GBP Checks

Check:

```text
Primary category matches highest-value service.
Secondary categories cover real services.
NAP is consistent with website/citations.
Website and appointment links work.
Business description is service + location focused.
Services are fully filled.
Photos are recent and real.
Reviews are active and relevant.
Q&A answers common buyer questions.
Posts are used for updates/offers/content.
UTM tracking exists.
```

---

## 4. Category Strategy

Primary category should match the main local intent.

Examples:

```text
Dentist
Dental Clinic
Emergency Dental Service
Orthodontist
Medical Clinic
Law Firm
Marketing Agency
Solar Energy Company
```

Do not add inaccurate categories just for ranking.

---

## 5. Services Strategy

Services should map to SEO landing pages.

Example dental:

```text
Dental Implants
Family Dentistry
Emergency Dentistry
Invisalign
Cosmetic Dentistry
Veneers
Teeth Whitening
Tooth Extraction
Wisdom Teeth Removal
```

Example AI agency:

```text
AI Automation Consulting
AI Agents Development
Chatbot Development
SEO Audit
AI Training
Business Process Automation
```

Each major GBP service should have a matching website page where possible.

---

## 6. Review Strategy

Audit:

```text
review count
rating
review velocity
keywords inside reviews
service-specific reviews
owner responses
negative review handling
competitor review gap
```

Action:

```text
Create review request workflow.
Ask for service-specific language naturally.
Respond to all reviews professionally.
Use reviews on matching service pages.
```

Never fake reviews.

```text
logo

team photos
location photos
service photos

class/event photos

recent posts
```

Post ideas:

```text

```

---

## 8. Q&A Strategy

Add/answer useful buyer questions:

```text
Do you offer same-day appointments?
Do you offer financing?
Which areas do you serve?
What services do you provide?
How can I book?
Do you offer online consultations/classes?
```

Answers should be honest and concise.

---

## 9. UTM Tracking

GBP links should use UTM parameters.

Recommended:

```text
?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp
```

For appointment link:

```text
?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp_appointment
```

Track:

```text
website clicks
appointment clicks
calls
form submissions
WhatsApp clicks
```
