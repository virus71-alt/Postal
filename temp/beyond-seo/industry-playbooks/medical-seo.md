# Medical SEO

Medical SEO requires high trust, clinical accuracy, expert review, disclaimers, and safe conversion language.

---

## Core Checks

Audit technical SEO, indexability, page architecture, keyword mapping, content depth, E-E-A-T, schema, backlinks, and conversion.

## Recommended Outputs

Create page map, content cluster, local/authority plan, conversion fixes, and 90-day roadmap.

## Warnings

Avoid generic content and fake guarantees. Use industry-specific trust proof.
