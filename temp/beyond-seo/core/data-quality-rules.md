# Data Quality Rules

Rules for judging and labeling data quality.

---

## Labels

Use Confirmed, Likely, Directional, Estimated, and Not Verified.

## Never Invent

Do not invent search volume, rankings, traffic, backlinks, DR/DA, GBP calls, or conversions.

## Tool Data

Apify community actors and scraped authority metrics are directional unless verified through official exports.
