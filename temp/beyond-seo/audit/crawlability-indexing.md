# Crawlability and Indexing Audit

Checks whether search engines can discover, crawl, render, and index important pages.

---

## Checks

Sitemap, robots.txt, noindex, canonical, status codes, internal links, JS rendering, blocked resources, orphan pages.

## Critical Issues

Money pages blocked, noindexed, canonicalized away, broken, or missing from crawl path.

## Output

URL, issue, evidence, impact, fix, priority.
