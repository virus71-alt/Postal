# Issue Prioritization

Defines how Beyond SEO ranks SEO issues by impact.

---

## Scoring

Impact 1-5, Effort 1-5, Confidence 1-5, Risk 1-5. Priority = Impact + Confidence - Effort - Risk.

## Labels

Must Fix Now, High Impact Next, Strategic Build, Monitor, Ignore for Now.

## Rule

Do not give 100 equal-priority tasks. Prioritization is the strategy.
