## Executive Summary

The website has one strong SEO pocket, but growth is limited by shallow money pages, narrow keyword coverage, weak internal linking, limited authority, and conversion gaps. The fastest path is to defend existing winners, build missing high-intent pages, strengthen local/authority signals, and improve conversion tracking.
