# 500-Query Growth Model

This file defines how Beyond SEO models lead/query targets such as 500 qualified queries per month.

The goal is to prevent fake SEO promises and connect rankings, traffic, local SEO, backlinks, and conversion.

---

## 1. Core Formula

```text
Required qualified visits/interactions = Target queries ÷ Conversion rate
```

Example:

```text
500 queries/month at 10% conversion = 5,000 qualified visits/interactions
500 queries/month at 7% conversion = 7,143 qualified visits/interactions
500 queries/month at 5% conversion = 10,000 qualified visits/interactions
```

---

## 2. Query Sources

Qualified queries may come from:

```text
Organic service pages
Organic blog/support pages
Google Business Profile
Direct/brand traffic
Referral/backlink traffic
Social-assisted traffic
Paid retargeting
Email/WhatsApp follow-up
```

SEO should not pretend all queries come only from blog traffic.

---

Use:


## 5. Growth Lever Split

Break target into levers:

```text
Service/money page traffic:
Support article traffic:
GBP/local actions:
Brand/direct:
Referral/backlink assisted:
CRO improvement:
```

Example:

```text
500 queries target
250 from service/local pages
100 from GBP/map visibility
75 from support articles
50 from brand/direct
25 from referral/PR
```

Adjust based on business type.

---

Estimate needed pages:

```text
Core money pages
Sub-service pages

Support articles

Case studies
```

```text
- family dentist city

- Invisalign city
```

---

## 7. Authority Requirement

If competitors have stronger authority, add authority work:

```text
Local citations
Niche links
Guest posts
Digital PR
Founder/expert mentions
Free article support posts
Directory profiles
Association links
Partner links
```

Do not recommend bulk spam links.

---

## 8. Conversion Requirement

If query target is high, CRO is mandatory.

Fix:

```text
CTA
Phone/WhatsApp
Forms
Booking/enrollment
Trust proof
Reviews
Pricing/financing
Mobile sticky CTA
Tracking
```

Explain:

```text
Doubling conversion rate can halve traffic required.
```
