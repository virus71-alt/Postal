# 30-Day SEO Action Plan

The 30-day plan is for urgent fixes, technical foundation, highest-value page improvements, and measurement setup.

The agent must not recommend 30 days of random activity. The first month must remove blockers and create the fastest path to measurable growth.

---

## 1. Purpose

The 30-day plan answers:

```text
What must be fixed immediately so SEO work can start producing results?
What pages should be improved first?
What tracking must be installed?
What quick wins can move rankings, traffic, or queries?
```

---

## 2. Inputs

Use:

```text
Full site audit
Technical audit
Keyword-to-page map
Competitor gap
Content gap
Local SEO audit
Backlink audit
AEO/GEO audit
Conversion audit
GSC/GA4/GBP data if available
```

---

## 3. 30-Day Priority Buckets

### Bucket 1: Measurement and Tracking

Fix first if missing:

```text
Google Search Console
GA4
Form submit tracking
Phone click tracking
WhatsApp click tracking
Booking/enrollment tracking
GBP UTM links
Thank-you pages or conversion events
```

If tracking is missing, the campaign cannot prove ROI.

### Bucket 2: Technical Blockers

Fix:

```text
Noindex on important pages
Blocked pages
Broken money pages
Wrong canonicals
JS-rendering problems
Duplicate metadata templates
Missing sitemap pages
404/5xx on important URLs
Broken forms/buttons
```

### Bucket 3: Existing Winners

Improve pages already ranking or close to ranking:

```text
Top 1-5 keywords: defend and improve conversion
Positions 4-20: fastest SEO wins
High-impression low-CTR pages: improve title/meta
High-traffic low-conversion pages: improve CTA/trust
```

### Bucket 4: Highest-Value Money Pages

Improve or create pages that directly support leads/sales:

```text
Main service page
Main course page
Location service page
Emergency/urgent page
Best existing commercial page
```

### Bucket 5: Local and GBP Quick Wins

For local businesses:

```text
GBP categories
Services/products
Photos
Reviews/review request flow
Q&A
Posts
Appointment link
UTM tracking
NAP consistency
```

### Bucket 6: Safe Authority Foundations

Start:

```text
Local citations
Association profiles
Partner/vendor links
Free article support posts from approved source library
Brand/entity profiles
High-quality directories
```

Do not start spam link buying in month one.

## 6. Rule

The 30-day plan should focus on execution, not theory.

If a task does not improve crawlability, indexability, ranking potential, conversion, local visibility, authority, or tracking, remove it.
